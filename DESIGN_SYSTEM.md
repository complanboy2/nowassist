# Design System - Minimal & Clean

## Colors
- **Primary (Sky Blue)**: #0EA5E9 (sky-500) - Primary buttons, links, accents
- **Primary Hover**: #0284C7 (sky-600)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (white)
- **Text Primary**: #111827 (gray-900)
- **Text Secondary**: #6B7280 (gray-500)
- **Border**: #E5E7EB (gray-200)
- **Success**: #10B981 (green-500)
- **Error**: #EF4444 (red-500)
- **Warning**: #F59E0B (amber-500)

## Typography
- **Heading 1**: text-3xl font-semibold text-gray-900
- **Heading 2**: text-xl font-semibold text-gray-900
- **Body**: text-base text-gray-700
- **Small**: text-sm text-gray-600
- **Line Height**: leading-relaxed (1.625)

## Spacing
- **Container Padding**: px-6 py-8
- **Section Gap**: space-y-6
- **Element Gap**: gap-4
- **Card Padding**: p-6

## Borders & Shadows
- **Card Border**: border border-gray-200 rounded-lg
- **No box shadows** (minimal design)
- **Focus Ring**: ring-2 ring-sky-500

## Buttons
- **Primary**: bg-sky-500 hover:bg-sky-600 text-white px-4 py-2 rounded-lg font-medium
- **Secondary**: border border-gray-300 text-gray-700 hover:bg-gray-50 px-4 py-2 rounded-lg
- **Text**: text-gray-700 hover:text-gray-900

## Layout
- **Max Width**: max-w-4xl (better for focused content)
- **Centered**: mx-auto
- **White Background**: bg-white
